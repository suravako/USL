## Why I built this

Every person who thinks seriously about things deserves a place on the internet that is truly theirs.

Not a profile. Not a feed. A **place** — with a URL you control, words that stay up, and ideas that can breathe.

This is mine.

## What you'll find here

I'll be writing about things that interest me: technology, creativity, the way ideas travel between people, and whatever else earns enough of my attention to become a post.

There's also an AI chatbot here, powered by Claude — feel free to use it to explore ideas, ask questions, or just see what it says.

## How to write your own post

Add a `.md` file to the `/posts` folder with this frontmatter at the top:

```
---
title: "Your Post Title"
date: "2026-04-25"
tag: "Category"
excerpt: "A short summary shown on the blog listing."
---
```

Then write your content in Markdown below the `---` line. It appears automatically on the blog.
